Data Processing Agreement

Required under DSGVO Art. 28 before we process any customer data on your behalf. Please read in full and confirm.

1. Parties and scope final text from legal

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") is entered into between Flow Pilates ("Controller") and STUDIO GmbH ("Processor"), governing the processing of personal data the Controller provides to the Processor through the STUDIO platform.

2. Subject matter and duration

The Processor processes contact data, booking history, payment status, and communication logs of the Controller's customers, exclusively for the purposes of CRM automation, marketing communications, and operational synchronisation between Eversports and GoHighLevel. The DPA remains in effect for the duration of the master service agreement.

3. Categories of data and data subjects

Categories processed: identifiers (name, email, phone), product / membership history, attendance records, communication content and timestamps, consent records.

4. Sub-processors

The Processor uses the following sub-processors: GoHighLevel (CRM hosting), Anthropic (AI inference, EU residency), AWS Frankfurt (data hosting), Doppler (secrets management). A 30-day notice precedes any sub-processor change.

5. Studio-attestation clause

The Controller represents and warrants that:

  • It has lawful authority to provide the Processor with Eversports admin authentication and to authorise the Processor to act as its delegate within the Eversports admin interface;
  • Such authorisation is not prohibited by the Controller's own contractual arrangements with Eversports;
  • The Controller shall promptly inform the Processor in writing if Eversports objects to or restricts the Controller's grant of delegated access;
  • The Controller indemnifies the Processor against any third-party claim arising from the Controller's grant of delegated access exceeding the Controller's authority.

6. Security measures

Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Access controls based on least privilege. Append-only consent audit log retained for 6 years. Breach notification within 24 hours to the Controller.

7. Data subject rights

The Processor assists the Controller in responding to data subject requests (access, rectification, erasure, portability) within the timelines required by DSGVO Art. 12–22.

8. Term and termination

Upon termination of the master service agreement, the Processor returns or deletes all personal data at the Controller's option within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or to defend legal claims.
